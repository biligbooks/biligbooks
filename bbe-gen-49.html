49:1 And Jacob sent for his sons, and said, Come together, all of you, so that I may give you news of your fate in future times.

49:1 雅各叫了他的儿子们来，说，你们都来聚集，我好把你们日后必遇的事告诉你们。

49:2 Come near, O sons of Jacob, and give ear to the words of Israel your father.

49:2 雅各的儿子们，你们要聚集而听，要听你们父亲以色列的话。

49:3 Reuben, you are my oldest son, the first-fruit of my strength, first in pride and first in power:

49:3 流便哪，你是我的长子，是我力量强壮的时候生的，本当大有尊荣，权力超众。

49:4 But because you were uncontrolled, the first place will not be yours; for you went up to your father's bed, even his bride-bed, and made it unclean.

49:4 但你放纵情欲，滚沸如水，必不得居首位。因为你上了你父亲的床，污秽了我的榻。

49:5 Simeon and Levi are brothers; deceit and force are their secret designs.

49:5 西缅和利未是弟兄。他们的刀剑是残忍的器具。

49:6 Take no part in their secrets, O my soul; keep far away, O my heart, from their meetings; for in their wrath they put men to death, and for their pleasure even oxen were wounded.

49:6 我的灵阿，不要与他们同谋。我的心哪，不要与他们联络。因为他们趁怒杀害人命，任意砍断牛腿大筋。

49:7 A curse on their passion for it was bitter; and on their wrath for it was cruel. I will let their heritage in Jacob be broken up, driving them from their places in Israel.

49:7 他们的怒气暴烈可咒。他们的忿恨残忍可诅。我要使他们分居在雅各家里，散住在以色列地中。

49:8 To you, Judah, will your brothers give praise: your hand will be on the neck of your haters; your father's sons will go down to the earth before you.

49:8 犹大阿，你弟兄们必赞美你。你手必掐住仇敌的颈项。你父亲的儿子们必向你下拜。

49:9 Judah is a young lion; like a lion full of meat you have become great, my son; now he takes his rest like a lion stretched out and like an old lion; by whom will his sleep be broken?

49:9 犹大是个小狮子。我儿阿，你抓了食便上去。你屈下身去，卧如公狮，蹲如母狮，谁敢惹你。

49:10 The rod of authority will not be taken from Judah, and he will not be without a law-giver, till he comes who has the right to it, and the peoples will put themselves under his rule.

49:10 圭必不离犹大，杖必不离他两脚之间，直等细罗（就是赐平安者）来到，万民都必归顺。

49:11 Knotting his ass's cord to the vine, and his young ass to the best vine; washing his robe in wine, and his clothing in the blood of grapes:

49:11 犹大把小驴拴在葡萄树上，把驴驹拴在美好的葡萄树上。他在葡萄酒中洗了衣服，在葡萄汁中洗了袍褂。

49:12 His eyes will be dark with wine, and his teeth white with milk.

49:12 他的眼睛必因酒红润。他的牙齿必因奶白亮。

49:13 The resting-place of Zebulun will be by the sea, and he will be a harbour for ships; the edge of his land will be by Zidon.

49:13 西布伦必住在海口，必成为停船的海口。他的境界必延到西顿。

49:14 Issachar is a strong ass stretched out among the flocks:

49:14 以萨迦是个强壮的驴，卧在羊圈之中。

49:15 And he saw that rest was good and the land was pleasing; so he let them put weights on his back and became a servant.

49:15 他以安静为佳，以肥地为美，便低肩背重，成为服苦的仆人。

49:16 Dan will be the judge of his people, as one of the tribes of Israel.

49:16 但必判断他的民，作以色列支派之一。

49:17 May Dan be a snake in the way, a horned snake by the road, biting the horse's foot so that the horseman has a fall.

49:17 但必作道上的蛇，路中的虺，咬伤马蹄，使骑马的坠落于后。

49:18 I have been waiting for your salvation, O Lord.

49:18 耶和华阿，我向来等候你的救恩。

49:19 Gad, an army will come against him, but he will come down on them in their flight.

49:19 迦得必被敌军追逼，他却要追逼他们的脚跟。

49:20 Asher's bread is fat; he gives delicate food for kings.

49:20 亚设之地必出肥美的粮食，且出君王的美味。

49:21 Naphtali is a roe let loose, giving fair young ones.

49:21 拿弗他利是被释放的母鹿。他出嘉美的言语。

49:22 Joseph is a young ox, whose steps are turned to the fountain;

49:22 约瑟是多结果子的树枝，是泉旁多结果的枝子。他的枝条探出墙外。

49:23 He was troubled by the archers; they sent out their arrows against him, cruelly wounding him:

49:23 弓箭手将他苦害，向他射箭，逼迫他。

49:24 But their bows were broken by a strong one, and the cords of their arms were cut by the Strength of Jacob, by the name of the Stone of Israel:

49:24 但他的弓仍旧坚硬。他的手健壮敏捷。这是因以色列的牧者，以色列的磐石就是雅各的大能者。

49:25 Even by the God of your father, who will be your help, and by the Ruler of all, who will make you full with blessings from heaven on high, blessings of the deep stretched out under the earth, blessings of the breasts and of the fertile body:

49:25 你父亲的神必帮助你。那全能者必将天上所有的福，地里所藏的福，以及生产乳养的福，都赐给你。

49:26 Blessings of sons, old and young, to the father: blessings of the oldest mountains and the fruit of the eternal hills: let them come on the head of Joseph, on the crown of him who was separate from his brothers.

49:26 你父亲所祝的福，胜过我祖先所祝的福，如永世的山岭，至极的边界。这些福必降在约瑟的头上，临列那与弟兄迥别之人的顶上。

49:27 Benjamin is a wolf, searching for meat: in the morning he takes his food, and in the evening he makes division of what he has taken.

49:27 便雅悯是个撕掠的狼，早晨要吃他所抓的，晚上要分他所夺的。

49:28 These are the twelve tribes of Israel: and these are the words their father said to them, blessing them; to every one he gave his blessing.

49:28 这一切是以色列的十二支派。这也是他们的父亲对他们所说的话，为他们所祝的福，都是按着各人的福分为他们祝福。

49:29 And he gave orders to them, saying, Put me to rest with my people and with my fathers, in the hollow of the rock in the field of Ephron the Hittite,

49:29 他又嘱咐他们说，我将要归到我列祖（原文作本民）那里，你们要将我葬在赫人以弗仑田间的洞里，与我祖我父在一处，

49:30 In the rock in the field of Machpelah, near Mamre in the land of Canaan, which Abraham got from Ephron the Hittite, to be his resting-place.

49:30 就是在迦南地幔利前，麦比拉田间的洞。那洞和田是亚伯拉罕向赫人以弗仑买来为业，作坟地的。

49:31 There Abraham and Sarah his wife were put to rest, and there they put Isaac and Rebekah his wife, and there I put Leah to rest.

49:31 他们在那里葬了亚伯拉罕和他妻子撒拉，又在那里葬了以撒和他妻子利百加。我也在那里葬了利亚。

49:32 In the rock in the field which was got for a price from the people of Heth.

49:32 那块田和田间的洞原是向赫人买的。

49:33 And when Jacob had come to the end of these words to his sons, stretching himself on his bed, he gave up his spirit, and went the way of his people.

49:33 雅各嘱咐众子已毕，就把脚收在床上，气绝而死，归到列祖（原文作本民）那里去了。
