36:1 And you, son of man, be a prophet about the mountains of Israel, and say, You mountains of Israel, give ear to the word of the Lord:

36:1 人子阿，你要对以色列山发预言说，以色列山哪，要听耶和华的话。

36:2 This is what the Lord has said: Because your hater has said against you, Aha! and, The old waste places are our heritage, we have taken them:

36:2 主耶和华如此说，因仇敌说，阿哈。这永久的山冈都归我们为业了。

36:3 For this cause be a prophet, and say, This is what the Lord has said: Because, even because they have been glad over you and put you to shame on every side, because you have become a heritage for the rest of the nations, and you are taken up on the lips of talkers and in the evil talk of the people:

36:3 所以要发预言说，主耶和华如此说，因为敌人使你荒凉，四围吞吃，好叫你归与其余的外邦人为业，并且多嘴多舌的人提起你来，百姓也说你有臭名。

36:4 For this reason, you mountains of Israel, give ear to the word of the Lord; this is what the Lord has said to the mountains and to the hills, to the streams and to the valleys, to the unpeopled wastes and to the towns where no one is living, from which the goods have been taken and which have been put to shame by the rest of the nations who are round about:

36:4 故此，以色列山要听主耶和华的话。大山小冈，水沟山谷，荒废之地，被弃之城，为四围其余的外邦人所占据，所讥刺的，

36:5 For this cause the Lord has said: Truly, in the heat of my bitter feeling I have said things against the rest of the nations and against all Edom, who have taken my land as a heritage for themselves with the joy of all their heart, and with bitter envy of soul have made attacks on it:

36:5 主耶和华对你们如此说，我真发愤恨如火，责备那其余的外邦人和以东的众人。他们快乐满怀，心存恨恶，将我的地归自己为业，又看为被弃的掠物。

36:6 For this cause be a prophet about the land of Israel, and say to the mountains and to the hills, to the streams and to the valleys, This is what the Lord has said: Truly, in my bitter feeling and in my wrath I have said these things, because you have undergone the shame of the nations:

36:6 所以，你要指着以色列地说预言，对大山小冈，水沟山谷说，主耶和华如此说，我发愤恨和忿怒说，因你们曾受外邦人的羞辱，

36:7 For this cause the Lord has said, See, I have taken an oath that the nations which are round about you are themselves to undergo the shame which they have put on you.

36:7 所以我起誓说，你们四围的外邦人总要担当自己的羞辱。这是主耶和华说的。

36:8 But you, O mountains of Israel, will put out your branches and give your fruit to my people Israel; for they are ready to come.

36:8 以色列山哪，你必发枝条，为我的民以色列结果子，因为他们快要来到。

36:9 For truly I am for you, and I will be turned to you, and you will be ploughed and planted:

36:9 看哪，我是帮助你的，也必向你转意，使你得以耕种。

36:10 And I will let your numbers be increased, all the children of Israel, even all of them: and the towns will be peopled and the waste places will have buildings;

36:10 我必使以色列全家的人数在你上面增多，城邑有人居住，荒场再被建造。

36:11 Man and beast will be increased in you, and they will have offspring and be fertile: I will make you thickly peopled as you were before, and will do more for you than at the first: and you will be certain that I am the Lord.

36:11 我必使人和牲畜在你上面加增。他们必生养众多。我要使你照旧有人居住，并要赐福与你比先前更多，你就知道我是耶和华。

36:12 Yes, I will have you walked on by the feet of men, even my people Israel; they will have you for a heritage and you will be theirs, and never again will you take their children from them.

36:12 我必使人，就是我的民以色列，行在你上面。他们必得你为业。你也不再使他们丧子。

36:13 This is what the Lord has said: Because they say to you, You, O land, are the destruction of men, causing loss of children to your nation;

36:13 主耶和华如此说，因为人对你说，你是吞吃人的，又使国民丧子。

36:14 For this reason you will no longer take the lives of men and will never again be the cause of loss of children to your nation, says the Lord.

36:14 所以主耶和华说，你必不再吞吃人，也不再使国民丧子。

36:15 And I will not let the shaming of the nations come to your ears, and no longer will you be looked down on by the peoples, says the Lord.

36:15 我使你不再听见各国的羞辱，不再受万民的辱骂，也不再使国民绊跌。这是主耶和华说的。

36:16 Then the word of the Lord came to me, saying,

36:16 耶和华的话又临到我说，

36:17 Son of man, when the children of Israel were living in their land, they made it unclean by their way and their acts: their way before me was as when a woman is unclean at the time when she is kept separate.

36:17 人子阿。以色列家住在本地的时候，在行动作为上玷污那地。他们的行为在我面前，好像正在经期的妇人那样污秽。

36:18 So I let loose my wrath on them because of those whom they had violently put to death in the land, and because they had made it unclean with their images:

36:18 所以我因他们在那地上流人的血，又因他们以偶像玷污那地，就把我的忿怒倾在他们身上。

36:19 And I sent them in flight among the nations and wandering through the countries: I was their judge, rewarding them for their way and their acts.

36:19 我将他们分散在列国，四散在列邦，按他们的行动作为惩罚他们。

36:20 And when they came among the nations, wherever they went, they made my holy name unclean, when it was said of them, These are the people of the Lord who have gone out from his land.

36:20 他们到了所去的列国，就使我的圣名被亵渎。因为人谈论他们说，这是耶和华的民，是从耶和华的地出来的。

36:21 But I had pity for my holy name which the children of Israel had made unclean wherever they went.

36:21 我却顾惜我的圣名，就是以色列家在所到的列国中所亵渎的。

36:22 For this cause say to the children of Israel, This is what the Lord has said: I am doing this, not because of you, O children of Israel, but because of my holy name, which you have made unclean among the nations wherever you went.

36:22 所以，你要对以色列家说，主耶和华如此说，以色列家阿，我行这事不是为你们，乃是为我的圣名，就是在你们到的列国中所亵渎的。

36:23 And I will make holy my great name which has been made unclean among the nations, which you have made unclean among them; and it will be clear to the nations that I am the Lord, says the Lord, when I make myself holy in you before their eyes.

36:23 我要使我的大名显为圣。这名在列国中已被亵渎，就是你们在他们中间所亵渎的。我在他们眼前，在你们身上显为圣的时候，他们就知道我是耶和华。这是主耶和华说的。

36:24 For I will take you out from among the nations, and get you together from all the countries, and take you into your land.

36:24 我必从各国收取你们，从列邦聚集你们，引导你们归回本地。

36:25 And I will put clean water on you so that you may be clean: from all your unclean ways and from all your images I will make you clean.

36:25 我必用清水洒在你们身上，你们就洁净了。我要洁净你们，使你们脱离一切的污秽，弃掉一切的偶像。

36:26 And I will give you a new heart and put a new spirit in you: I will take away the heart of stone from your flesh, and give you a heart of flesh.

36:26 我也要赐给你们一个新心，将新灵放在你们里面，又从你们的肉体中除掉石心，赐给你们肉心。

36:27 And I will put my spirit in you, causing you to be guided by my rules, and you will keep my orders and do them.

36:27 我必将我的灵放在你们里面，使你们顺从我的律例，谨守遵行我的典章。

36:28 So that you may go on living in the land which I gave to your fathers; and you will be to me a people, and I will be to you a God.

36:28 你们必住在我所赐给你们列祖之地。你们要作我的子民，我要作你们的神。

36:29 And I will make you free from all your unclean ways: and at my voice the grain will come up and be increased, and I will not let you be short of food.

36:29 我必救你们脱离一切的污秽，也必命五谷丰登，不使你们遭遇饥荒。

36:30 And I will make the tree give more fruit and the field fuller produce, and no longer will you be shamed among the nations for need of food.

36:30 我必使树木多结果子，田地多出土产，好叫你们不再因饥荒受外邦人的讥诮。

36:31 And at the memory of your evil ways and your wrongdoings, you will have bitter hate for yourselves because of your evil-doings and your disgusting ways, O children of Israel.

36:31 那时，你们必追想你们的恶行和你们不善的作为，就因你们的罪孽和可憎的事厌恶自己。

36:32 Not because of you am I doing it, says the Lord; let it be clear to you, and be shamed and made low because of your ways, O children of Israel.

36:32 主耶和华说，你们要知道，我这样行不是为你们。以色列家阿，当为自己的行为抱愧蒙羞。

36:33 This is what the Lord has said: In the day when I make you clean from all your evil-doings I will let the towns be peopled and there will be building on the waste places.

36:33 主耶和华如此说，我洁净你们，使你们脱离一切罪孽的日子，必使城邑有人居住，荒场再被建造。

36:34 And the land which was waste will be farmed, in place of being a waste in the eyes of everyone who went by.

36:34 过路的人虽看为荒废之地，现今这荒废之地仍得耕种。

36:35 And they will say, This land which was waste has become like the garden of Eden; and the towns which were unpeopled and wasted and pulled down are walled and peopled.

36:35 他们必说，这先前为荒废之地，现在成如伊甸园。这荒废凄凉，毁坏的城邑现在坚固有人居住。

36:36 Then the rest of the nations round about you will be certain that I the Lord am the builder of the places which were pulled down and the planter of that which was waste: I the Lord have said it, and I will do it.

36:36 那时，在你们四围其余的外邦人必知道我耶和华修造那毁坏之处，培植那荒废之地。我耶和华说过，也必成就。

36:37 This is what the Lord has said: The children of Israel will again make prayer to me for this, that I may do it for them; I will make them increased with men like a flock.

36:37 主耶和华如此说，我要加增以色列家的人数，多如羊群。他们必为这事向我求问，我要给他们成就。

36:38 Like sheep for the offerings, like the sheep of Jerusalem at her fixed feasts, so the unpeopled towns will be made full of men: and they will be certain that I am the Lord.

36:38 耶路撒冷在守节作祭物所献的羊群怎样多，照样，荒凉的城邑必被人群充满。他们就知道我是耶和华。
