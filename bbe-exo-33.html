33:1 And the Lord said to Moses, Go forward from this place, you and the people whom you have taken up out of the land of Egypt, to that land about which I made an oath to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob, saying, To your seed will I give it.

33:1 耶和华吩咐摩西说，我曾起誓应许亚伯拉罕，以撒，雅各说，要将迦南地赐给你的后裔。现在你和你从埃及地所领出来的百姓，要从这里往那地去。

33:2 And I will send an angel before you, driving out the Canaanite and the Amorite and the Hittite and the Perizzite and the Hivite and the Jebusite:

33:2 我要差遣使者在你前面，撵出迦南人，亚摩利人，赫人，比利洗人，希未人，耶布斯人，

33:3 Go up to that land flowing with milk and honey; but I will not go up among you, for you are a stiff-necked people, for fear that I send destruction on you while you are on the way.

33:3 领你到那流奶与蜜之地。我自己不同你们上去，因为你们是硬着颈项的百姓，恐怕我在路上把你们灭绝。

33:4 Hearing this bad news the people were full of grief, and no one put on his ornaments.

33:4 百姓听见这凶信就悲哀，也没有人佩戴妆饰。

33:5 And the Lord said to Moses, Say to the children of Israel, You are a stiff-necked people: if I come among you, even for a minute, I will send destruction on you; so take off all your ornaments, so that I may see what to do with you.

33:5 耶和华对摩西说，你告诉以色列人说，耶和华说，你们是硬着颈项的百姓，我若一霎时临到你们中间，必灭绝你们。现在你们要把身上的妆饰摘下来，使我可以知道怎样待你们。

33:6 So the children of Israel took off their ornaments at Mount Horeb, and did not put them on again.

33:6 以色列人从住何烈山以后，就把身上的妆饰摘得干净。

33:7 Now it was Moses' way to put up the Tent of meeting outside the tent-circle, at some distance away; giving it the name of The Tent of meeting. And everyone desiring to make his prayer to the Lord went to the Tent of meeting outside the tent-circle.

33:7 摩西素常将帐棚支搭在营外，离营却远，他称这帐棚为会幕。凡求问耶和华的，就到营外的会幕那里去。

33:8 And whenever Moses went out to the Tent of meeting, all the people got up and everyone went to the door of his tent, looking after Moses till he went inside the Tent.

33:8 当摩西出营到会幕去的时候，百姓就都起来，各人站在自己帐棚的门口，望着摩西，直等到他进了会幕。

33:9 And whenever Moses went into the Tent, the pillar of cloud came down, and took its place by the door of the Tent, as long as the Lord was talking with Moses.

33:9 摩西进会幕的时候，云柱降下来，立在会幕的门前，耶和华便与摩西说话。

33:10 And all the people saw the cloud at the door of the Tent, and they went down on their faces, everyone at the door of his tent.

33:10 众百姓看见云柱立在会幕门前，就都起来，各人在自己帐棚的门口下拜。

33:11 And the Lord had talk with Moses face to face, as a man may have talk with his friend. And when Moses came back to the tents, his servant, the young man Joshua, the son of Nun, did not come away from the Tent.

33:11 耶和华与摩西面对面说话，好像人与朋友说话一般。摩西转到营里去，惟有他的帮手，一个少年人嫩的儿子约书亚，不离开会幕。

33:12 And Moses said to the Lord, See, you say to me, Be this people's guide on their journey, but you have not made clear to me whom you will send with me. But you have said, I have knowledge of you by name, and you have grace in my eyes.

33:12 摩西对耶和华说，你吩咐我说，将这百姓领上去，却没有叫我知道你要打发谁与我同去，只说，我按你的名认识你，你在我眼前也蒙了恩。

33:13 If then I have grace in your eyes, let me see your ways, so that I may have knowledge of you and be certain of your grace; and my prayer is that you will keep in mind that this nation is your people.

33:13 我如今若在你眼前蒙恩，求你将你的道指示我，使我可以认识你，好在你眼前蒙恩。求你想到这民是你的民。

33:14 And he said, I myself will go with you and give you rest.

33:14 耶和华说，我必亲自和你同去，使你得安息。

33:15 And Moses said, If you yourself are not going with us, do not send us on from here.

33:15 摩西说，你若不亲自和我同去，就不要把我们从这里领上去。

33:16 For is not the fact of your going with us the sign that I and this people have grace in your eyes, so that we, that is, I and your people, are separate from all other people on the face of the earth?

33:16 人在何事上得以知道我和你的百姓在你眼前蒙恩呢？岂不是因你与我们同去，使我和你的百姓与地上的万民有分别吗？

33:17 And the Lord said to Moses, I will do as you say: for you have grace in my eyes, and I have knowledge of you by your name.

33:17 耶和华对摩西说，你这所求的我也要行，因为你在我眼前蒙了恩，并且我按你的名认识你。

33:18 And Moses said, O Lord, let me see your glory.

33:18 摩西说，求你显出你的荣耀给我看。

33:19 And he said, I will make all the light of my being come before you, and will make clear to you what I am; I will be kind to those to whom I will be kind, and have mercy on those on whom I will have mercy.

33:19 耶和华说，我要显我一切的恩慈，在你面前经过，宣告我的名。我要恩待谁就恩待谁，要怜悯谁就怜悯谁，

33:20 But it is not possible for you to see my face, for no man may see me and still go on living.

33:20 又说，你不能看见我的面，因为人见我的面不能存活。

33:21 And the Lord said, See, there is a place near me, and you may take your place on the rock:

33:21 耶和华说，看哪，在我这里有地方，你要站在磐石上。

33:22 And when my glory goes by, I will put you in a hole in the rock, covering you with my hand till I have gone past:

33:22 我的荣耀经过的时候，我必将你放在磐石穴中，用我的手遮掩你，等我过去，

33:23 Then I will take away my hand, and you will see my back: but my face is not to be seen.

33:23 然后我要将我的手收回，你就得见我的背，却不得见我的面。
