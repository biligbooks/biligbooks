34:1 And the Lord said to Moses, Make two other stones like the first two; and I will put on them the words which were on the first stones, which were broken by you.

34:1 耶和华吩咐摩西说，你要凿出两块石版，和先前你摔碎的那版一样，其上的字我要写在这版上。

34:2 And be ready by the morning, and come up on Mount Sinai, and come before me there in the morning, on the top of the mountain.

34:2 明日早晨，你要预备好了，上西乃山，在山顶上站在我面前。

34:3 No one is to come up with you, and let no man be seen anywhere on the mountain; let no flocks or herds come near to get their food at its foot.

34:3 谁也不可和你一同上去，遍山都不可有人，在山根也不可叫羊群牛群吃草。

34:4 So Moses got two stones cut like the first; and early in the morning he went up Mount Sinai, as the Lord had said, with the two stones in his hand.

34:4 摩西就凿出两块石版，和先前的一样。清晨起来，照耶和华所吩咐的上西乃山去，手里拿着两块石版。

34:5 And the Lord came down in the cloud and took his place by the side of Moses, and Moses gave worship to the name of the Lord.

34:5 耶和华在云中降临，和摩西一同站在那里，宣告耶和华的名。

34:6 And the Lord went past before his eyes, saying, The Lord, the Lord, a God full of pity and grace, slow to wrath and great in mercy and faith;

34:6 耶和华在他面前宣告说，耶和华，耶和华，是有怜悯有恩典的神，不轻易发怒，并有丰盛的慈爱和诚实。

34:7 Having mercy on thousands, overlooking evil and wrongdoing and sin; he will not let wrongdoers go free, but will send punishment on children for the sins of their fathers, and on their children's children to the third and fourth generation.

34:7 为千万人存留慈爱，赦免罪孽，过犯，和罪恶，万不以有罪的为无罪，必追讨他的罪，自父及子，直到三，四代。

34:8 Then Moses quickly went down on his face in worship.

34:8 摩西急忙伏地下拜，

34:9 And he said, If now I have grace in your eyes, let the Lord go among us, for this is a stiff-necked people, and give us forgiveness for our wrongdoing and our sin, and take us for your heritage.

34:9 说，主阿，我若在你眼前蒙恩，求你在我们中间同行，因为这是硬着颈项的百姓。又求你赦免我们的罪孽和罪恶，以我们为你的产业。

34:10 And the Lord said, See, this is what I will undertake: before the eyes of your people I will do wonders, such as have not been done in all the earth or in any nation: and all your people will see the work of the Lord, for what I am about to do for you is greatly to be feared.

34:10 耶和华说，我要立约，要在百姓面前行奇妙的事，是在遍地万国中所未曾行的。在你四围的外邦人就要看见耶和华的作为，因我向你所行的是可畏惧的事。

34:11 Take care to do the orders which I give you today; I will send out from before you the Amorite and the Canaanite and the Hittite and the Perizzite and the Hivite and the Jebusite.

34:11 我今天所吩咐你的，你要谨守。我要从你面前撵出亚摩利人，迦南人，赫人，比利洗人，希未人，耶布斯人。

34:12 But take care, and do not make any agreement with the people of the land where you are going, for it will be a cause of sin to you.

34:12 你要谨慎，不可与你所去那地的居民立约，恐怕成为你们中间的网罗，

34:13 But their altars are to be overturned and their pillars broken and their images cut down:

34:13 却要拆毁他们的祭坛，打碎他们的柱像，砍下他们的木偶。

34:14 For you are to be worshippers of no other god: for the Lord is a God who will not give his honour to another.

34:14 不可敬拜别神，因为耶和华是忌邪的神，名为忌邪者。

34:15 So see that you make no agreement with the people of the land, and do not go after their gods, or take part in their offerings, or be guests at their feasts,

34:15 只怕你与那地的居民立约，百姓随从他们的神，就行邪淫，祭祀他们的神，有人叫你，你便吃他的祭物，

34:16 Or take their daughters for your sons; for when their daughters give worship before their gods, they will make your sons take part with them.

34:16 又为你的儿子娶他们的女儿为妻，他们的女儿随从他们的神，就行邪淫，使你的儿子也随从他们的神行邪淫。

34:17 Make for yourselves no gods of metal.

34:17 不可为自己铸造神像。

34:18 Keep the feast of unleavened bread; for seven days your food is to be bread without leaven, as I gave you orders, at the regular time in the month Abib; for in that month you came out of Egypt.

34:18 你要守除酵节，照我所吩咐你的，在亚笔月内所定的日期吃无酵饼七天，因为你是这亚笔月内出了埃及。

34:19 Every first male child is mine; the first male birth of your cattle, the first male of every ox and sheep.

34:19 凡头生的都是我的，一切牲畜头生的，无论是牛是羊，公的都是我的。

34:20 A lamb may be given in payment for the young of an ass, but if you will not make payment for it, its neck will have to be broken. For all the first of your sons you are to make payment. No one is to come before me without an offering.

34:20 头生的驴要用羊羔代赎，若不代赎就要打折它的颈项。凡头生的儿子都要赎出来。谁也不可空手朝见我。

34:21 Six days let work be done, but on the seventh day take your rest: at ploughing time and at the grain-cutting you are to have a day for rest.

34:21 你六日要作工，第七日要安息，虽在耕种收割的时候也要安息。

34:22 And you are to keep the feast of weeks when you get in the first-fruits of the grain, and the feast at the turn of the year when you take in the produce of your fields.

34:22 在收割初熟麦子的时候要守七七节，又在年底要守收藏节。

34:23 Three times in the year let all your males come before the Lord, the God of Israel.

34:23 你们一切男丁要一年三次朝见主耶和华以色列的神。

34:24 For I will send out the nations before you and make wide the limits of your land; and no man will make an attempt to take your land while you go up to give worship to the Lord, three times in the year.

34:24 我要从你面前赶出外邦人，扩张你的境界。你一年三次上去朝见耶和华你神的时候，必没有人贪慕你的地土。

34:25 No leaven is to be offered with the blood of my offering, and the offering of the Passover feast may not be kept till the morning.

34:25 你不可将我祭物的血和有酵的饼一同献上。逾越节的祭物也不可留到早晨。

34:26 Take the first-fruits of your land as an offering to the house of the Lord your God. Let not the young goat be cooked in its mother's milk

34:26 地里首先初熟之物要送到耶和华你神的殿。不可用山羊羔母的奶煮山羊羔。

34:27 And the Lord said to Moses, Put all these words in writing; for on them is based the agreement which I will make with you.

34:27 耶和华吩咐摩西说，你要将这些话写上，因为我是按这话与你和以色列人立约。

34:28 And for forty days and forty nights Moses was there with the Lord, and in that time he had no food or drink. And he put in writing on the stones the words of the agreement, the ten rules of the law.

34:28 摩西在耶和华那里四十昼夜，也不吃饭也不喝水。耶和华将这约的话，就是十条诫，写在两块版上。

34:29 Now when Moses came down from Mount Sinai, with the two stones in his hand, he was not conscious that his face was shining because of his talk with God.

34:29 摩西手里拿着两块法版下西乃山的时候，不知道自己的面皮因耶和华和他说话就发了光。

34:30 But when Aaron and all the children of Israel saw Moses, and the shining of his face, they would not come near him for fear.

34:30 亚伦和以色列众人看见摩西的面皮发光就怕挨近他。

34:31 Then Moses sent for them; and Aaron, with the chiefs of the people, came to him; and Moses had talk with them.

34:31 摩西叫他们来，于是亚伦和会众的官长都到他那里去，摩西就与他们说话。

34:32 And later, all the children of Israel came near, and he gave them all the orders which the Lord had given him on Mount Sinai.

34:32 随后以色列众人都近前来，他就把耶和华在西乃山与他所说的一切话都吩咐他们。

34:33 And at the end of his talk with them, Moses put a veil over his face.

34:33 摩西与他们说完了话就用帕子蒙上脸。

34:34 But whenever Moses went in before the Lord to have talk with him, he took off the veil till he came out. And whenever he came out he said to the children of Israel what he had been ordered to say;

34:34 但摩西进到耶和华面前与他说话就揭去帕子，及至出来的时候便将耶和华所吩咐的告诉以色列人。

34:35 And the children of Israel saw that the face of Moses was shining: so Moses put the veil over his face again till he went to the Lord.

34:35 以色列人看见摩西的面皮发光。摩西又用帕子蒙上脸，等到他进去与耶和华说话，就揭去帕子。
