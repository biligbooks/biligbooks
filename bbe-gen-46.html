46:1 And Israel went on his journey with all he had, and came to Beer-sheba, where he made offerings to the God of his father Isaac.

46:1 以色列带着一切所有的，起身来到别是巴，就献祭给他父亲以撒的神。

46:2 And God said to Israel in a night-vision, Jacob, Jacob. And he said, Here am I.

46:2 夜间，神在异象中对以色列说，雅各。雅各。他说，我在这里。

46:3 And he said, I am God, the God of your father: go down to Egypt without fear, for I will make a great nation of you there:

46:3 神说，我是神，就是你父亲的神。你下埃及去不要害怕，因为我必使你在那里成为大族。

46:4 I will go down with you to Egypt, and I will see that you come back again, and at your death Joseph will put his hands on your eyes.

46:4 我要和你同下埃及去，也必定带你上来。约瑟必给你送终（原文作将手按在你的眼睛上）。

46:5 Then Jacob went on from Beer-sheba; and the sons of Jacob took their father and their little ones and their wives in the carts which Pharaoh had sent for them.

46:5 雅各就从别是巴起行。以色列的儿子们使他们的父亲雅各和他们的妻子，儿女都坐在法老为雅各送来的车上。

46:6 And they took their cattle and all the goods which they had got in the land of Canaan, and came to Egypt, even Jacob and all his seed:

46:6 他们又带着迦南地所得的牲畜，货财来到埃及。雅各和他的一切子孙都一同来了，

46:7 His sons and his sons' sons, his daughters and his daughters' sons and all his family he took with him into Egypt.

46:7 雅各把他的儿子，孙子，女儿，孙女，并他的子子孙孙，一同带到埃及。

46:8 And these are the names of the children of Israel who came into Egypt, even Jacob and all his sons: Reuben, Jacob's oldest son;

46:8 来到埃及的以色列人名字记在下面。雅各和他的儿孙，雅各的长子是流便。

46:9 And the sons of Reuben: Hanoch and Pallu and Hezron and Carmi;

46:9 流便的儿子是哈诺，法路，希斯伦，迦米。

46:10 And the sons of Simeon: Jemuel and Jamin and Ohad and Jachin and Zohar and Shaul, the son of a woman of Canaan;

46:10 西缅的儿子是耶母利，雅悯，阿辖，雅斤，琐辖，还有迦南女子所生的扫罗。

46:11 And the sons of Levi: Gershon, Kohath, and Merari;

46:11 利未的儿子是革顺，哥辖，米拉利。

46:12 And the sons of Judah: Er and Onan and Shelah and Perez and Zerah: but Er and Onan had come to their death in the land of Canaan; and the sons of Perez were Hezron and Hamul.

46:12 犹大的儿子是珥，俄南，示拉，法勒斯，谢拉。惟有珥与俄南死在迦南地。法勒斯的儿子是希斯伦，哈母勒。

46:13 And the sons of Issachar: Tola and Puah and Job and Shimron;

46:13 以萨迦的儿子是陀拉，普瓦，约伯，伸仑。

46:14 And the sons of Zebulun: Sered and Elon and Jahleel;

46:14 西布伦的儿子是西烈，以伦，雅利。

46:15 All these, together with his daughter Dinah, were the children of Leah, whom Jacob had by her in Paddan-aram; they were thirty-three in number.

46:15 这是利亚在巴旦亚兰给雅各所生的儿子，还有女儿底拿。儿孙共三十三人。

46:16 And the sons of Gad: Ziphion and Haggi, Shuni and Ezbon, Eri and Arodi and Areli;

46:16 迦得的儿子是洗非芸，哈基，书尼，以斯本，以利，亚罗底，亚列利。

46:17 And the sons of Asher: Jimnah and Ishvah and Ishvi and Beriah, and Sarah, their sister; and the sons of Beriah: Heber and Malchiel.

46:17 亚设的儿子是音拿，亦施瓦，亦施韦，比利亚，还有他们的妹子西拉。比利亚的儿子是希别，玛结。

46:18 These are the children of Zilpah, whom Laban gave to his daughter Leah, and Jacob had these sixteen children by her.

46:18 这是拉班给他女儿利亚的婢女悉帕从雅各所生的儿孙，共有十六人。

46:19 The sons of Jacob's wife Rachel: Joseph and Benjamin.

46:19 雅各之妻拉结的儿子是约瑟和便雅悯。

46:20 And Joseph had Manasseh and Ephraim in the land of Egypt, by Asenath, the daughter of Poti-phera, priest of On.

46:20 约瑟在埃及地生了玛拿西和以法莲，就是安城的祭司波提非拉的女儿亚西纳给约瑟生的。

46:21 And the sons of Benjamin were Belah and Becher and Ashbel, Gera and Naaman, Ehi and Rosh, Muppim and Huppim and Ard.

46:21 便雅悯的儿子是比拉，比结，亚实别，基拉，乃幔，以希，罗实，母平，户平，亚勒。

46:22 All these were the children of Rachel whom Jacob had by her, fourteen persons.

46:22 这是拉结给雅各所生的儿孙，共有十四人。

46:23 And the son of Dan was Hushim.

46:23 但的儿子是户伸。

46:24 And the sons of Naphtali: Jahzeel and Guni and Jezer and Shillem.

46:24 拿弗他利的儿子是雅薛，沽尼，耶色，示冷。

46:25 These were the children of Bilhah, whom Laban gave to his daughter Rachel, seven persons.

46:25 这是拉班给他女儿拉结的婢女辟拉从雅各所生的儿孙，共有七人。

46:26 All the persons who came with Jacob into Egypt, the offspring of his body, were sixty-six, without taking into account the wives of Jacob's sons.

46:26 那与雅各同到埃及的，除了他儿妇之外，凡从他所生的，共有六十六人。

46:27 And the sons of Joseph whom he had in Egypt were two. Seventy persons of the family of Jacob came into Egypt.

46:27 还有约瑟在埃及所生的两个儿子。雅各家来到埃及的共有七十人。

46:28 Now he had sent Judah before him to Goshen, to get word from Joseph; and so they came to the land of Goshen.

46:28 雅各打发犹大先去见约瑟，请派人引路往歌珊去。于是他们来到歌珊地。

46:29 And Joseph got his carriage ready and went to Goshen for the meeting with his father; and when he came before him, he put his arms round his neck, weeping.

46:29 约瑟套车往歌珊去，迎接他父亲以色列，及至见了面，就伏在父亲的颈项上，哭了许久。

46:30 And Israel said to Joseph, Now that I have seen you living again, I am ready for death.

46:30 以色列对约瑟说，我既得见你的面，知道你还在，就是死我也甘心。

46:31 And Joseph said to his brothers and to his father's people, I will go and give the news to Pharaoh, and say to him, My brothers and my father's people, from the land of Canaan, have come to me;

46:31 约瑟对他的弟兄和他父的全家说，我要上去告诉法老，对他说，我的弟兄和我父的全家从前在迦南地，现今都到我这里来了。

46:32 And these men are keepers of sheep and owners of cattle, and have with them their flocks and their herds and all they have.

46:32 他们本是牧羊的人，以养牲畜为业。他们把羊群牛群和一切所有的都带来了。

46:33 Now when Pharaoh sends for you and says, What is your business?

46:33 等法老召你们的时候，问你们说，你们以何事为业。

46:34 You are to say, Your servants have been keepers of cattle from our early days up to now, like our fathers; in this way you will be able to have the land of Goshen for yourselves; because keepers of sheep are unclean in the eyes of the Egyptians.

46:34 你们要说，你的仆人，从幼年直到如今，都以养牲畜为业，连我们的祖宗也都以此为业。这样，你们可以住在歌珊地，因为凡牧羊的都被埃及人所厌恶。
