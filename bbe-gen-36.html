36:1 Now these are the generations of Esau, that is to say, Edom.

36:1 以扫就是以东，他的后代记在下面。

36:2 Esau's wives were women of Canaan: Adah, the daughter of Elon the Hittite, and Oholibamah, the daughter of Anah, the daughter of Zibeon the Hivite,

36:2 以扫娶迦南的女子为妻，就是赫人以伦的女儿亚大和希未人祭便的孙女，亚拿的女儿阿何利巴玛，

36:3 And Basemath, Ishmael's daughter, the sister of Nebaioth.

36:3 又娶了以实玛利的女儿，尼拜约的妹子巴实抹。

36:4 Adah had a son Eliphaz; and Basemath was the mother of Reuel;

36:4 亚大给以扫生了以利法。巴实抹生了流珥。

36:5 Oholibamah was the mother of Jeush, Jalam, and Korah; these are the sons of Esau, whose birth took place in the land of Canaan.

36:5 阿何利巴玛生了耶乌施，雅兰，可拉。这都是以扫的儿子，是在迦南地生的。

36:6 Esau took his wives and his sons and his daughters, and all the people of his house, and his beasts and his cattle and all his goods which he had got together in the land of Canaan, and went into the land of Seir, away from his brother Jacob.

36:6 以扫带着他的妻子，儿女，与家中一切的人口，并他的牛羊，牲畜，和一切货财，就是他在迦南地所得的，往别处去，离了他兄弟雅各。

36:7 For their wealth was so great that the land was not wide enough for the two of them and all their cattle.

36:7 因为二人的财物群畜甚多，寄居的地方容不下他们，所以不能同居。

36:8 So Esau made his living-place in the hill-country of Seir (Esau is Edom).

36:8 于是以扫住在西珥山里。以扫就是以东。

36:9 And these are the generations of Esau, the father of the Edomites in the hill-country of Seir:

36:9 以扫是西珥山里以东人的始祖，他的后代记在下面。

36:10 These are the names of Esau's sons: Eliphaz, the son of Esau's wife Adah, and Reuel, the son of Esau's wife Basemath.

36:10 以扫众子的名字如下。以扫的妻子亚大生以利法。以扫的妻子巴实抹生流珥。

36:11 The sons of Eliphaz were Teman, Omar, Zepho, Gatam, and Kenaz.

36:11 以利法的儿子是提幔，阿抹，洗玻，迦坦，基纳斯。

36:12 And Eliphaz, the son of Esau, had connection with a woman named Timna, who gave birth to Amalek: all these were the children of Esau's wife Adah.

36:12 亭纳是以扫儿子以利法的妾。她给以利法生了亚玛力。这是以扫的妻子亚大的子孙。

36:13 And these are the sons of Reuel: Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah: they were the children of Esau's wife Basemath.

36:13 流珥的儿子是拿哈，谢拉，沙玛，米撒。这是以扫妻子巴实抹的子孙。

36:14 And these are the sons of Esau's wife Oholibamah, the daughter of Anah, the daughter of Zibeon: she was the mother of Jeush, Jalam, and Korah.

36:14 以扫的妻子阿何利巴玛是祭便的孙女，亚拿的女儿。她给以扫生了耶乌施，雅兰，可拉。

36:15 These were the chiefs among the sons of Esau: the sons of Eliphaz, Esau's first son: Teman, Omar, Zepho, Kenaz,

36:15 以扫子孙中作族长的记在下面。以扫的长子以利法的子孙中，有提幔族长，阿抹族长，洗玻族长，基纳斯族长，

36:16 Korah, Gatam, Amalek: all these were chiefs in the land of Edom, the offspring of Eliphaz, the seed of Adah.

36:16 可拉族长，迦坦族长，亚玛力族长。这是在以东地从以利法所出的族长，都是亚大的子孙。

36:17 And these are the sons of Esau's son Reuel: Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, Mizzah: these were the chiefs of Reuel in the land of Edom, the children of Esau's wife Basemath.

36:17 以扫的儿子流珥的子孙中，有拿哈族长，谢拉族长，沙玛族长，米撒族长。这是在以东地从流珥所出的族长，都是以扫妻子巴实抹的子孙。

36:18 And these are the sons of Esau's wife Oholibamah: Jeush, Jalam, and Korah: these were the chiefs who came from Esau's wife Oholibamah, daughter of Anah.

36:18 以扫的妻子阿何利巴玛的子孙中，有耶乌施族长，雅兰族长，可拉族长。这是从以扫妻子，亚拿的女儿，阿何利巴玛子孙中所出的族长。

36:19 These were the sons of Esau (that is, Edom), and these were their chiefs.

36:19 以上的族长都是以扫的子孙。以扫就是以东。

36:20 These are the sons of Seir the Horite who were living in that country; Lotan, Shobal, Zibeon, Anah,

36:20 那地原有的居民何利人西珥的子孙记在下面，就是罗坍，朔巴，祭便，亚拿，

36:21 Dishon, Ezer, and Dishan: these are the chiefs of the Horites, offspring of Seir in the land of Edom.

36:21 底顺，以察，底珊。这是从以东地的何利人西珥子孙中所出的族长。

36:22 The children of Lotan were Hori and Hemam; Lotan's sister was Timna.

36:22 罗坍的儿子是何利，希幔。罗坍的妹子是亭纳。

36:23 And these are the children of Shobal: Alvan, Manahath, Ebal, Shepho, and Onam.

36:23 朔巴的儿子是亚勒文，玛拿辖，以巴录，示玻，阿南。

36:24 And these are the children of Zibeon: Aiah and Anah; that same Anah who made the discovery of the water-springs in the waste land, when he was looking after the asses of his father Zibeon.

36:24 祭便的儿子是亚雅，亚拿（当时在旷野放他父亲祭便的驴，遇着温泉的，就是这亚拿）。

36:25 And these are the children of Anah: Dishon and Oholibamah his daughter.

36:25 亚拿的儿子是底顺。亚拿的女儿是阿何利巴玛。

36:26 These are the children of Dishon: Hemdan, Eshban, Ithran, and Keran.

36:26 底顺的儿子是欣但，伊是班，益兰，基兰。

36:27 These are the children of Ezer: Bilhan, Zaavan, and Akan.

36:27 以察的儿子是辟罕，撒番，亚干。

36:28 These are the children of Dishan: Uz and Aran.

36:28 底珊的儿子是乌斯，亚兰。

36:29 These were the Horite chiefs: Lotan, Shobal, Zibeon, Anah,

36:29 从何利人所出的族长记在下面，就是罗坍族长，朔巴族长，祭便族长，亚拿族长，

36:30 Dishon, Ezer, and Dishan. Such were the Horite chiefs in their order in the land of Seir.

36:30 底顺族长，以察族长，底珊族长。这是从何利人所出的族长，都在西珥地，按着宗族作族长。

36:31 And these are the kings who were ruling in the land of Edom before there was any king over the children of Israel.

36:31 以色列人未有君王治理以先，在以东地作王的记在下面。

36:32 Bela, son of Beor, was king in Edom, and the name of his chief town was Dinhabah.

36:32 比珥的儿子比拉在以东作王，他的京城名叫亭哈巴。

36:33 At his death, Jobab, son of Zerah of Bozrah, became king in his place.

36:33 比拉死了，波斯拉人谢拉的儿子约巴接续他作王。

36:34 And at the death of Jobab, Husham, from the country of the Temanites, became king in his place.

36:34 约巴死了，提幔地的人户珊接续他作王。

36:35 And at the death of Husham, Hadad, son of Bedad, who overcame the Midianites in the field of Moab, became king; his chief town was named Avith.

36:35 户珊死了，比达的儿子哈达接续他作王。这哈达就是在摩押地杀败米甸人的，他的京城名叫亚未得。

36:36 And at the death of Hadad, Samlah of Masrekah became king.

36:36 哈达死了，玛士利加人桑拉接续他作王。

36:37 And at the death of Samlah, Shaul of Rehoboth by the River became king in his place.

36:37 桑拉死了，大河边的利河伯人扫罗接续他作王。

36:38 And at the death of Shaul, Baal-hanan, son of Achbor, became king.

36:38 扫罗死了，亚革波的儿子巴勒哈南接续他作王。

36:39 And at the death of Baal-hanan, Hadar became king in his place; his chief town was named Pau, and his wife's name was Mehetabel; she was the daughter of Matred, the daughter of Me-zahab.

36:39 亚革波的儿子巴勒哈南死了，哈达接续他作王，他的京城名叫巴乌。他的妻子名叫米希他别，是米萨合的孙女，玛特列的女儿。

36:40 These are the names of the chiefs of Esau in the order of their families and their places: Timna, Alvah, Jetheth,

36:40 从以扫所出的族长，按着他们的宗族，住处，名字记在下面，就是亭纳族长，亚勒瓦族长，耶帖族长，

36:41 Oholibamah, Elah, Pinon,

36:41 阿何利巴玛族长，以拉族长，比嫩族长，

36:42 Kenaz, Teman, Mibzar,

36:42 基纳斯族长，提幔族长，米比萨族长，

36:43 Magdiel, Iram; these are the Edomite chiefs, in their places in their heritage; this is Esau, the father of the Edomites.

36:43 玛基叠族长，以兰族长。这是以东人在所得为业的地上，按着他们的住处，所有的族长都是以东人的始祖以扫的后代。
